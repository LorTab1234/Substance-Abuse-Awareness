Initiatives

SAA run a variety of initiatives in order to support the community in times of need. If you are interested, make sure you sign up to these initiatives and we will notify you if there is anything on in the community.






What's On

Hoops with SAA

Get ready to shoot some hoops at Michael Clarke Recreational Centre. Bring your shoes, ball and mates to shoot some hoops and play some games with SAA. Entry fee will be $2 and all proceeds will go to those who have been affected by Substance Abuse.

Sign up now

SAA Seminar on Substance Abuse

Come down to St Francis Catholic College for a Seminar on Substance Abuse. Food and Drinks will be provided.

Sign up now

SAA Barbeque Festival

Bring your friends and family for a Barbeque down at Collimore Park. Food and Drinks will be provided with a Gold Coin Donation. All proceeds will go to funding healthcare for those affected by Substance Abuse.

Sign up now
